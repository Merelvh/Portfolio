My name is Merel

I’m a UX and UI designer based in the Netherlands focusing on app, web and mobile interfaces. In addition, I developed fundamental skills in front-end programming to be able to make my creations come to life.

My career started as a speech therapist, which I did for 5 years before switching to design. My time as a speech therapist greatly expanded my communications skills which transfer well when creating clear and easy to use user experiences.

Besides getting enthusiastic about using my creativity in my work, I’m a travel-enthusiast through and through. I have been maintaining my own travel based website since 2016 and have gained experience using a variety of tools like Wordpress, HTML, SCSS, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD and Figma.
