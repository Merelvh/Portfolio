My name is Merel

I’m a UX and UI designer based in the Netherlands focusing on app, web and mobile interfaces. In addition, I developed fundamental skills in front-end programming to be able to make my creations come to life. That's why I decided to code this portfolio website.

My career started as a speech therapist, which I did for 5 years before switching to design. My time as a speech therapist greatly expanded my communications skills that transfer well when creating clear and easy to use user experiences.

Besides getting enthusiastic about using my creativity in my work, I’m a travel-enthusiast through and through. I have been maintaining my own travel based website since 2016 and have gained experience using a variety of tools like Wordpress, HTML, SCSS, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD and Figma.

My philosophy

Doing something I enjoy that gives me satisfaction.

This has been my guiding philosophy during my career switch, to both give me satisfaction in my work and use my skills to the best of my ability. I decided to let go of certainty to follow a new path to find my passion.

Following guidelines but always thinking out of the box.

Every design is different and needs a different approach. I believe that it is important to empathize with all types of users and to avoid tunnel vision by taking a new perspective. This also means taking space from designing to gain fresh perspectives and inspiration.
