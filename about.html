My name is Merel

I’m a self-taught UX and UI designer based in the Netherlands focusing on app, web and mobile interfaces. In addition, I am beginning to develop my skills in front-end programming.

My career started as a speech therapist, which I did for 5 years before switching to design. My time as a speech therapist greatly expanded my communications skills which transfer well when creating clear and easy to use user experiences.

Besides getting enthusiastic about using my creativity in my work, I’m a travel-enthusiast through and through. I have been maintaining my own travel based website since 2016 and have gained experience using a variety of tools like wordpress, html, css, food and travel photography.

  • Wireframing
  • Low and high fidelity prototyping
  • Layout and typography
